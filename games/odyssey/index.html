The Return

Twenty years. A war. Monsters. Gods at odds with each other and a sea that wanted you dead. And in the end the proof was not a weapon or a god's favour — it was a bed rooted in a living tree, a secret that could only belong to you.

Penelope knew. She had always known. She just needed you to say it.
